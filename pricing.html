Frequently Asked Questions

What's a "shard"?

A shard is an independent, fully-replicated Roomzin cluster. Large OTAs typically have one shard per region (e.g., Paris, New York, Tokyo).

Can I use Roomzin in production without a license?

No. The binary is fully functional for evaluation, but a commercial license is required for production use under BUSL-1.1.

What's included in the license?

Full feature set, software updates, email support, and the right to use Roomzin in production for one year.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or open-source projects?

Yes — contact us with your use case and we'll discuss options.

How do I get started?

Download the binary from our Download page, run it in evaluation mode, and contact us when you're ready for a commercial license.
